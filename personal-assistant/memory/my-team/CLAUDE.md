# My Team

Parent: [../CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md)

## What Lives Here

`memory/my-team/` is {{NAME}}'s people-management workspace: durable knowledge about the people {{NAME}} directly manages. Use this folder for 1:1 notes, performance-review evidence, career-ladder positioning, progression plans, coaching themes, reporting-line context, and management-relevant highlights.

Do **not** use this folder as an operational project tracker. Current tasks, execution status, and delivery blockers belong in [`TASKS.md`](../../TASKS.md) or [`project-management/`](../project-management/CLAUDE.md). Only pull a work item into `my-team/` when it becomes management evidence: a strong contribution, recurring behavior, coaching need, performance concern, career-growth opportunity, or feedback input.

## What Belongs Here

- **1:1 notes:** recurring topics, commitments, follow-ups, feedback delivered, feedback received.
- **Career ladder:** current level hypothesis, target level, scope expectations, evidence, gaps.
- **Performance reviews:** evidence packets, strengths, growth areas, examples, manager narrative, peer/stakeholder input.
- **Coaching themes:** patterns to revisit over time — communication, autonomy, ownership, judgment, collaboration.
- **Management-relevant highlights:** specific work only when it reveals capability, impact, behavior, growth, or risk.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Active project checklists and per-issue execution status.
- Weekly delivery updates and roadmap milestones (unless used as performance evidence).
- People outside {{NAME}}'s reporting line → [`collaborators/`](../collaborators/CLAUDE.md).
- Generic work notes that do not affect management, feedback, review, or career decisions.

## Suggested Person-Page Shape

1. **Role / reporting context** — current relationship to {{NAME}} and reporting-line state.
2. **Career ladder** — current level hypothesis, target level, evidence, gaps.
3. **Performance signals** — durable examples, not task logs.
4. **1:1 notes** — dated entries with follow-ups.
5. **Feedback / coaching themes** — what to reinforce, what to correct, what to observe.
6. **Review packet** — raw material for performance reviews.
7. **Links** — operational work elsewhere.
