# Collaborators

Parent: [../CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md)

## What Lives Here

`memory/collaborators/` is the durable-knowledge home for **people and teams outside {{NAME}}'s own team and reporting line** that {{NAME}} engages with: teams {{NAME}} advises or consults for, peer leaders, leaders from other areas, cross-functional stakeholders, and any external collaborators.

When a meeting, note, or source brings durable context about someone or some team that isn't on {{NAME}}'s own team and isn't itself a planning surface, that knowledge lands here as a team page or a person page.

## What Belongs Here

- **Other teams {{NAME}} advises or consults for** whose context recurs.
- **Peer leaders** {{NAME}} collaborates with on cross-team initiatives.
- **Leaders from other areas** (e.g. Product, Marketing, Ops, Finance) whose context recurs.
- **Stakeholders** whose decisions or constraints shape {{NAME}}'s work.
- **External people** (advisors, vendors, peers at other organizations) when the relationship is load-bearing.

Each entry should be either a **team page** (durable reference for the group) or a **person page** (only when the relationship is load-bearing enough to warrant one; otherwise capture the person inline on the relevant team page).

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Durable knowledge that isn't about external collaborators → back in the flat wiki catalog in [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md) (Projects / Concepts / Preferences).
- Active follow-ups and execution → [`TASKS.md`](../../TASKS.md).
