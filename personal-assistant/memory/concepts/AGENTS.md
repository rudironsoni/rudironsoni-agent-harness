# Concepts

Parent: [../AGENTS.md](../AGENTS.md)

## What Lives Here

`memory/concepts/` is the durable-knowledge home for **reusable methodologies, frameworks, mental models, training programs, and playbooks** that inform how {{NAME}} thinks and works. Concepts are not tied to a specific person, team, or project — they're knowledge that can be applied across many contexts.

## What Belongs Here

- **Methodologies** {{NAME}} uses or studies.
- **Reusable training programs and playbooks**.
- **Frameworks and patterns** that recur and are worth a durable, canonical write-up.
- **Mental models** that show up across multiple engagements.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Anything tied to a specific person or team → [`my-team/`](../my-team/AGENTS.md) or back in the flat wiki catalog in [`../AGENTS.md`](../AGENTS.md).
- Active follow-ups, deadlines, or commitments → [`TASKS.md`](../../TASKS.md).
- Roadmap or execution planning → [`project-management/`](../project-management/AGENTS.md).
