# Project Management

Parent: [../CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md)

## What Lives Here

`memory/project-management/` is {{NAME}}'s project-management and execution-planning surface. It holds the operational view of what is in flight: a working roadmap or milestone pool, board snapshots, dashboard or metrics pages, and any planning artifacts that ground *what is actually being delivered*.

This is the operational counterpart to the strategic and durable knowledge elsewhere in the wiki: it answers "what's in flight and what's next", not "why" or "who".

## What Belongs Here

- A working **roadmap** or milestone pool that ladders longer-range direction down to concrete steps.
- **Board snapshots** or backlog summaries (e.g. exported from an external tracker, if you use one).
- **Dashboard, metrics, or KPI** definitions and structure.
- **Execution-planning** pages: rollout plans, initiative briefs, status snapshots.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Durable methodology or framework knowledge → [`concepts/`](../concepts/CLAUDE.md).
- Management evidence about people → [`my-team/`](../my-team/CLAUDE.md).
- Active personal follow-ups and commitments → [`TASKS.md`](../../TASKS.md).
