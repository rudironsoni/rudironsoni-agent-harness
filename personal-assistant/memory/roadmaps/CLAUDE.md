# Roadmaps

Parent: [../CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md)

## What Lives Here

`memory/roadmaps/` holds quarterly or otherwise time-boxed roadmaps and plans for {{NAME}}'s area or team. Each page captures the planned bets, themes, and priorities for a given period, so the direction is legible at a glance and traceable over time.

A roadmap here is the bridge between long-range vision and the operational backlog: it consumes both and produces a period's concrete plan. It reconciles aspiration (where things are heading and why) with current execution capacity (what is actually in flight, see [`project-management/`](../project-management/CLAUDE.md)).

## What Belongs Here

- **Time-boxed roadmaps** — one page per quarter or planning period.
- **Bet and theme summaries** that frame a period's focus.
- **Plan snapshots** that translate longer-range direction into a period's concrete priorities.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Live execution status, board snapshots, and dashboards → [`project-management/`](../project-management/CLAUDE.md).
- Durable methodology or framework knowledge → [`concepts/`](../concepts/CLAUDE.md).
- Active personal follow-ups and commitments → [`TASKS.md`](../../TASKS.md).
